## Your job: customize 2 slides

You only need to edit two slides with local context. Everything else stays as-is.

| Slide | What to add |
|-------|------------|
| **Local intro slide** | Your chapter name, city, your name as host |
| **Local events slide** | Any upcoming local AIC events you want to promote |

Make a copy of the deck before editing (`File → Make a copy`). Do not edit the original.

## Delivery notes

- The presentation is ~15–20 minutes
- You don't need to read it verbatim — use it as a guide
- End with a strong close into the breakout: "The question we're here to ask tonight is..."
- Close the full event by directing people to [hiai.com](https://hiai.com) to take the pledge
