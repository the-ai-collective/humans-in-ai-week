# Tuesday: Research Roundtable — HQ Opening Script

**Total time**: ~5 minutes. Read this aloud at the start of your event. Every HAIW event worldwide opens with the same shared message.

---

## 1. Shared opening (~2 min)

> Welcome to Humans in AI Week.
>
> Right now, in over 200 cities across 50+ countries, people just like you are gathering to have the same conversation: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?
>
> This isn't a conference. It's a convening — the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.
>
> The AI Collective built this because 0.01% of the population is building a technology that will shape the other 99.99%. Everyone deserves a seat at that table.
>
> Tonight, this is your seat.

---

## 2. Format-specific bridge (~1 min)

> Tonight is a Research Roundtable. We've brought together [NUMBER] researchers and thinkers who spend their days studying the questions most of us are just starting to ask.
>
> This isn't a lecture — it's a conversation. They'll share what they're seeing from the frontier of AI research, and then we'll open it up to you.
>
> The best insights tonight will come from the questions you ask.

---

## 3. Localization prompt (~1 min)

*[CHAPTER LEAD: Insert 2–3 sentences about your city's research community. Any universities, labs, or research institutions working on AI? What local research stories are people talking about?]*

---

## 4. Handoff (~30 sec)

> Thank you to [SPONSOR NAME] for tonight, to [VENUE] for the space, and to our panelists for sharing their time and expertise.
>
> Let's get into it. [MODERATOR NAME], take it away.
