# Saturday: Hackathon — HQ Opening Script

**Total time**: ~5 minutes. Read this aloud at the start of your event. Every HAIW event worldwide opens with the same shared message.

---

## 1. Shared opening (~2 min)

> Welcome to Humans in AI Week.
>
> Right now, in over 200 cities across 50+ countries, people just like you are gathering to have the same conversation: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?
>
> This isn't a conference. It's a convening — the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.
>
> The AI Collective built this because 0.01% of the population is building a technology that will shape the other 99.99%. Everyone deserves a seat at that table.
>
> Today, this is your seat.

---

## 2. Format-specific bridge (~1 min)

> Today is a Hackathon — the biggest, longest, most ambitious format in Humans in AI Week.
>
> Over the next 8 hours, you'll form teams, pick a challenge, and build something real with AI. By 4 PM, every team will demo what they've built.
>
> It doesn't have to be perfect. It has to be real.
>
> Our mentors are here to help, and every skill in this room matters — not just coding. Design, storytelling, domain expertise — great teams need all of it.

---

## 3. Localization prompt (~1 min)

*[CHAPTER LEAD: Announce today's theme/challenge. Explain why you chose it and how it connects to your city. Introduce the mentors and judges briefly.]*

---

## 4. Handoff (~30 sec)

> Thank you to [SPONSOR NAME] for fueling today, to [VENUE] for giving us the space, and to our mentors: [MENTOR NAMES].
>
> Here's how team formation works: anyone with an idea has 60 seconds to pitch it. Then you vote with your feet — walk to the idea that excites you. Let's hear the pitches.
