# Friday: Workshop — HQ Opening Script

**Total time**: ~5 minutes. Read this aloud at the start of your event. Every HAIW event worldwide opens with the same shared message.

---

## 1. Shared opening (~2 min)

> Welcome to Humans in AI Week.
>
> Right now, in over 200 cities across 50+ countries, people just like you are gathering to have the same conversation: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?
>
> This isn't a conference. It's a convening — the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.
>
> The AI Collective built this because 0.01% of the population is building a technology that will shape the other 99.99%. Everyone deserves a seat at that table.
>
> Tonight, this is your seat.

---

## 2. Format-specific bridge (~1 min)

> Tonight is a Workshop. By the end of this session, every person in this room will have built something with AI. Not watched someone else do it — done it yourself.
>
> We'll start with a guided walkthrough, then you'll build your own project. No experience required. Our helpers are here to make sure nobody gets left behind.

---

## 3. Localization prompt (~1 min)

*[CHAPTER LEAD: Insert 2–3 sentences about what you'll be building tonight and why you chose this tool. Connect it to what's happening in your city — what problems could this solve locally?]*

---

## 4. Handoff (~30 sec)

> Thank you to [SPONSOR NAME] and [VENUE].
>
> Quick logistics: Wi-Fi network is [NETWORK NAME], password is [PASSWORD]. If you haven't installed the tool yet, raise your hand and a helper will come to you.
>
> [INSTRUCTOR NAME], take it away.
