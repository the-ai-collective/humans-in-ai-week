# Monday: Demo Night — HQ Opening Script

**Total time**: ~5 minutes. Read this aloud at the start of your event. Every HAIW event worldwide opens with the same shared message.

---

## 1. Shared opening (~2 min)

> Welcome to Humans in AI Week.
>
> Right now, in over 200 cities across 50+ countries, people just like you are gathering to have the same conversation: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?
>
> This isn't a conference. It's a convening — the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.
>
> The AI Collective built this because 0.01% of the population is building a technology that will shape the other 99.99%. Everyone deserves a seat at that table.
>
> Tonight, this is your seat.

---

## 2. Format-specific bridge (~1 min)

> Tonight is Demo Night. You're about to see [NUMBER] live demos from people in your community who are building with AI right now. Not pitching. Not theorizing. Building.
>
> After the demos, you'll vote on your favorites, and we'll bring the top presenters up for a deeper conversation.
>
> This is the best way to see what AI actually looks like when real people use it to solve real problems.

---

## 3. Localization prompt (~1 min)

*[CHAPTER LEAD: Insert 2–3 sentences about your city's builder community. What's being built here? What industries are being transformed? Why does tonight matter for YOUR city?]*

---

## 4. Handoff (~30 sec)

> Before we start — thank you to [SPONSOR NAME] for making tonight possible, to [VENUE] for hosting us, and to every one of you for being here.
>
> Let's see what [CITY] is building. First demo — [FIRST PRESENTER NAME], you're up.
