# Monday: Demo Night — Agenda

**Duration**: 2.5 hours | **Ideal size**: 50–150 attendees

---

## Run of show

| Time | Segment | Duration | Notes |
|------|---------|----------|-------|
| 6:00 PM | **Doors open** | 30 min | Registration, networking, refreshments |
| 6:30 PM | **HQ Opening** | 5 min | Read from [`hq-opening.md`](./hq-opening.md) — non-negotiable |
| 6:35 PM | **Demo format intro** | 5 min | Explain rules: 5 min per demo, 2 min Q&A, audience votes at end |
| 6:40 PM | **Demo block** | 55 min | 5–8 demos. Each presenter: 5 min demo + 2 min audience Q&A. Strict timer |
| 7:35 PM | **Audience voting** | 10 min | Paper ballots or live poll (Slido, Mentimeter). Top 3 advance |
| 7:45 PM | **Panel with top demos** | 20 min | Moderated conversation with top-voted presenters. "What problem are you solving?" |
| 8:05 PM | **Closing** | 5 min | Thank sponsors, announce winners, share how to stay connected |
| 8:10 PM | **Networking** | 20 min | Open mingling. Presenters available for 1:1 conversations |
| 8:30 PM | **Wrap** | — | Venue closes |

---

## Pre-event setup checklist

- [ ] Test projector and HDMI/USB-C adapters with multiple laptop types
- [ ] Set up demo stations if presenters need table space
- [ ] Prepare timer app visible to presenters (phone timer on a stand works)
- [ ] Print voting ballots or set up live polling tool
- [ ] Test audio system and microphone
- [ ] Set up registration table with name tags

---

## Equipment requirements

- Projector + screen (large enough for the room)
- HDMI and USB-C adapters (bring at least 2 of each)
- Timer visible to presenters (countdown app on a tablet or phone)
- Microphone (wireless preferred so presenters can move)
- Speaker or bell for time's-up signal

---

## Required roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| MC / host | Introduces presenters, manages transitions, runs voting and panel |
| Timekeeper | Enforces 5-min demo + 2-min Q&A. Visible countdown. No exceptions |
| Vote counter | Collects and tallies ballots (or manages digital poll) |
| A/V helper | Manages projector, adapters, sound. Helps presenters connect |
| Photographer | Minimum 10 photos: demos in action, crowd reactions, winners |

---

## Topic track overlay

Demo Night works best with **Track 4 (Building with AI)** or **Track 5 (How to Be AI Native)**. After the demos, steer the panel discussion toward your chosen track's themes. The demos ARE the content — the track shapes the conversation around them.
