# Wednesday: Discussion Meetup — HQ Opening Script

**Total time**: ~5 minutes. Read this aloud at the start of your event. Every HAIW event worldwide opens with the same shared message.

---

## 1. Shared opening (~2 min)

> Welcome to Humans in AI Week.
>
> Right now, in over 200 cities across 50+ countries, people just like you are gathering to have the same conversation: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?
>
> This isn't a conference. It's a convening — the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.
>
> The AI Collective built this because 0.01% of the population is building a technology that will shape the other 99.99%. Everyone deserves a seat at that table.
>
> Tonight, this is your seat.

---

## 2. Format-specific bridge (~1 min)

> Tonight is a Discussion Meetup — the most intimate format in Humans in AI Week. There's no stage, no panel, no audience. We're all participants.
>
> We'll have three rounds of discussion, each building on the last. The only rule: listen as much as you speak.
>
> The best conversations happen when everyone in the room brings something.

---

## 3. Localization prompt (~1 min)

*[CHAPTER LEAD: Insert 2–3 sentences about why this conversation matters in your city. What's happening locally with AI? What are people feeling? What makes your community's perspective unique?]*

---

## 4. Handoff (~30 sec)

> Thank you to [SPONSOR NAME] and [VENUE] for making this possible.
>
> Ground rules are simple: this is a conversation, not a debate. All perspectives welcome. Chatham House Rule — share what was said, not who said it.
>
> Let's begin. Our first question tonight is: [READ ROUND 1 QUESTION].
