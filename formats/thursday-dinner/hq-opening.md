# Thursday: Dinner — HQ Opening Script

**Total time**: ~5 minutes. Read this aloud at the start of your event. Every HAIW event worldwide opens with the same shared message. Deliver during the first course.

---

## 1. Shared opening (~2 min)

> Welcome to Humans in AI Week.
>
> Right now, in over 200 cities across 50+ countries, people just like you are gathering to have the same conversation: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?
>
> This isn't a conference. It's a convening — the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.
>
> The AI Collective built this because 0.01% of the population is building a technology that will shape the other 99.99%. Everyone deserves a seat at that table.
>
> Tonight, this is your seat.

---

## 2. Format-specific bridge (~1 min)

> Tonight is a little different from a typical meetup. We're having dinner together — and the conversation is the main course.
>
> You've been seated with people you probably don't know yet. That's by design. On your table, you'll find three prompt cards. Over the next hour, your table will work through each one.
>
> At the end, one person from each table will share your group's most interesting insight with the room.

---

## 3. Localization prompt (~1 min)

*[CHAPTER LEAD: Insert 2–3 sentences about why you chose this format and what makes tonight's group special. Acknowledge the diversity in the room — industries, backgrounds, perspectives.]*

---

## 4. Handoff (~30 sec)

> Thank you to [SPONSOR NAME] for making tonight's dinner possible, and to [VENUE] for hosting us.
>
> Enjoy your first course, introduce yourselves at your table, and when you're ready — flip over prompt card number one. Bon appetit.
