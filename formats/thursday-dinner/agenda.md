# Thursday: Dinner — Agenda

**Duration**: 3 hours | **Ideal size**: 20–80 attendees

The highest-cost, most curated format. Food is the medium. Conversation is the point.

---

## Run of show

| Time | Segment | Duration | Notes |
|------|---------|----------|-------|
| 6:30 PM | **Arrival + drinks** | 30 min | Welcome drinks, name tags with table assignments, mingling |
| 7:00 PM | **HQ Opening** (during first course) | 5 min | Read from [`hq-opening.md`](./hq-opening.md) while first course is being served |
| 7:05 PM | **Table introductions** | 15 min | Each person at their table: name, what they do, one sentence on their relationship with AI |
| 7:20 PM | **Discussion Round 1** | 20 min | Prompt card #1 on each table. Table discusses, one person captures key points |
| 7:40 PM | **Discussion Round 2** | 20 min | Prompt card #2. Deeper question. Main course served during discussion |
| 8:00 PM | **Discussion Round 3** | 20 min | Prompt card #3. Action-oriented: "What should [CITY] do about this?" |
| 8:20 PM | **Report-backs** | 15 min | One person per table shares their table's top insight (1–2 min each) |
| 8:35 PM | **Dessert + open networking** | 50 min | Informal mixing. People move between tables |
| 9:25 PM | **Closing remarks** | 5 min | Thank sponsors, highlight key themes, share follow-up info |
| 9:30 PM | **Depart** | — | |

---

## Pre-event setup checklist

- [ ] Confirm final headcount with caterer (48 hours before)
- [ ] Print table assignments and post at entrance
- [ ] Print prompt cards — 3 per table, on cardstock
- [ ] Set up name tags with table numbers
- [ ] Arrange tables for 6–8 people each (not 10 — too many kills discussion)
- [ ] Test audio for HQ Opening and report-backs (single mic)
- [ ] Coordinate with catering on course timing (serve between rounds, not during)
- [ ] Place pen + notepad at each table for note-taker

---

## Equipment requirements

- Round tables seating 6–8 each
- Printed prompt cards (cardstock recommended, large font)
- Name tags with table assignments
- Single microphone for HQ Opening and report-backs
- Pen + notepad per table for note-taker

---

## Required roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Host | MC for opening, closing, and report-backs. Keeps energy and timing |
| Table facilitators | 1 per table — recruit volunteers, brief them 30 min before. Guide table discussion |
| Caterer liaison | Coordinates course timing with discussion rounds |
| Photographer | Captures table conversations, food, ambiance, report-backs |

---

## Topic track overlay

Dinner works with **any track**. The prompt cards replace standard discussion questions. Write 3 prompt cards that progress from broad → specific → action-oriented. Use your chosen track's questions as a starting point.

**Strongest pairings:**
- **Track 2** (AI and the Future of Work) — everyone at the table has a job AI might change
- **Track 6** (AI in Your City & Industry) — local context makes dinner conversation personal
- **Track 1** (What It Means to Be Human in the AI Era) — philosophical, great over food and wine
