## How breakout groups use questions

- Each group gets 2–3 questions to work through in ~45 minutes
- No right answers — the goal is to surface perspectives and spark reflection
- A facilitator (or just a volunteer at the table) keeps things moving
- See [`content/facilitation-guide.md`](./facilitation-guide.md) for guidance on running the groups

## Adding your own questions

Chapter leads can add locally relevant questions. Good questions for this format:

- Are open-ended (no yes/no answer)
- Connect the global AI conversation to something concrete in your city or industry
- Don't require technical knowledge to engage with
- Generate more questions than answers

Submit questions by opening a PR to this file, or send them to aj@aicollective.com to be considered for the HQ set.
