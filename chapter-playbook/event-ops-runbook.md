# Event Ops Runbook

> **Google Doc version**: [Event Ops Runbook (L-02)](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1e12_VLENAvX4WG86DuR8z38jcI7Ip0J3/edit)

## Overview

This is your complete guide to hosting a Humans in AI Week event in your city. Every AIC chapter is expected to host one public event during June 1–7, 2026.

**What you're hosting**: A public event using one of 6 formats — Demo Night, Research Roundtable, Discussion Meetup, Dinner, Workshop, or Hackathon. See [`/formats/`](../formats/) for detailed agendas and best practices for each.

**Your goal**: Get 50–200 people in a room having a real conversation about AI. Not a lecture. Not a pitch. A conversation.

## Your responsibilities as chapter lead

- [ ] Secure a venue (free or sponsor-covered)
- [ ] Choose your [event format](../formats/) and optionally a [topic track](../content/discussion-guides/)
- [ ] Recruit 2–4 panelists or discussion facilitators
- [ ] Register your event on Luma
- [ ] Promote locally using provided marketing assets
- [ ] Secure at least 1 local sponsor to cover costs (target: $0 out-of-pocket)
- [ ] Execute the event during June 1–7
- [ ] Submit a post-event report within 48 hours

## Venue guidelines

**Ideal venues** (aim for free):
- University lecture halls or auditoriums
- Coworking spaces (WeWork, local equivalents)
- Corporate event spaces (sponsor-provided)
- Libraries, community centers
- Tech company offices

**Requirements**:
- Capacity: 50–200 people
- A/V: microphone, projector/screen (for any slides or livestream)
- Accessible location with public transit access
- Wi-Fi (for livestream component)

**Cost target**: $0. Use the [Local Sponsor Pitch](../sponsorship/local-sponsor-pitch.md) to get venue + catering covered.

## Budget framework

| Item | Estimated cost | Coverage |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Venue | $0–$500 | Sponsor or in-kind |
| Food & beverage | $200–$800 | Sponsor |
| A/V equipment | $0–$300 | Venue-provided or sponsor |
| Marketing/printing | $0–$100 | Optional |
| **Total** | **$200–$1,700** | **Target: 100% sponsor-covered** |

**Format-specific costs**: Discussion Meetup can run at $0–200. Demo Night, Research Roundtable, and Workshop: $200–500. Dinner: $500–3,000 (highest per-person cost). Hackathon: $800–3,000 (full-day venue + catering). See each format's `best-practices.md` for detailed budget notes.

## Panelist / speaker recruitment

You need 2–4 people for a panel or 1–2 skilled facilitators for a roundtable.

**Who to recruit**:
- Local AI practitioners (engineers, product managers, researchers)
- Academics working on AI ethics, policy, or technology
- Business leaders navigating AI adoption
- Community voices — educators, artists, healthcare workers, journalists
- People *affected by* AI, not just people *building* AI

**Diversity matters**: Aim for diverse perspectives across gender, race, industry, and AI experience level. The whole point is that this conversation includes everyone.

**How to ask**: Use the [Speaker Invite Email](../marketing/email-templates/speaker-invite.md) template.

## Day-of checklist

### 2 hours before

- [ ] Arrive at venue, test A/V
- [ ] Set up registration/check-in (name tags optional but recommended)
- [ ] Brief panelists/facilitators on format and timing
- [ ] Test livestream connection (if applicable)
- [ ] Set up signage and any sponsor branding

### 30 minutes before

- [ ] Doors open
- [ ] Welcome music/ambiance (optional)
- [ ] Greet early arrivals

### During event

- [ ] Follow your [Run of Show](./run-of-show-template.md)
- [ ] Take photos (minimum 10 — crowd shots, panelists, candids)
- [ ] Capture 2–3 notable quotes
- [ ] Monitor time — keep segments on track

### After event

- [ ] Thank attendees, panelists, sponsors, and venue
- [ ] Share photos on social with #HumansInAIWeek
- [ ] Submit [Post-Event Report](./post-event-report-template.md) within 48 hours

## Post-event reporting

Every chapter must submit a report within 48 hours. This data powers the global narrative — "200 cities said X" is how we earn media coverage and demonstrate impact.
