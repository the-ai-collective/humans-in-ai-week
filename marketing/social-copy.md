# Social Copy

Ready-to-use copy for chapter leads promoting their local event. Replace all `[BRACKETED]` fields with your local details.

---

## Carousel copy

Use these as slides or captions when promoting the event to different audiences.

**Want to Partner?**
Back the largest decentralized AI gathering ever organized. Partners get exposure and direct presence across 100+ cities and 200,000+ members.

**Want to Attend?**
Find the event in your city the week of June 1–7 and add your voice to the global time capsule. Share your voice to help humanity collectively decide where this technology goes next.

**Want to Host?**
Run Humans in AI Week in your city and put your community on the global record. Hosts make history through leading local programming and collecting time capsule contributions alongside 100+ cities globally.
