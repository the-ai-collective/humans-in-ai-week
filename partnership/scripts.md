# Partnership Scripts

For chapter leads pitching local community organizations, companies, or media partners to co-promote or co-host Humans in AI Week events.

---

## What a community partner is

A community partner co-promotes the event to their audience. They don't pay anything — they just help spread the word and in return get recognition as a partner.

Good targets: universities, professional associations, tech communities, local media, meetup groups, accelerators.

---

## Verbal pitch

> "We're hosting a Humans in AI Week event in [city] on [date] as part of a global week of simultaneous AI conversations — 100+ cities, 50+ countries. The question we're gathering around is: what does it mean to be human in the AI era?
>
> We'd love [Organization] to be a community partner — basically just co-promote it to your community. We'd list you as a partner in our event page and materials. Your people would be a great fit for this conversation.
>
> Would you be open to sharing it with your community?"

---

## Email: Community partner ask

**Subject:** Community partner — Humans in AI Week in [city]?

Hi [Name],

I'm hosting a Humans in AI Week event in [city] on [date]. It's part of a global week happening June 1–7 in 100+ cities — the largest coordinated public conversation about AI ever organized.

I'd love [Organization] to be a community partner. The ask is simple: share the event with your community. In exchange, we'll feature [Organization] as a partner on the event page and in our outreach.

The event is free to attend and open to everyone — the audience will include [describe: founders, researchers, operators, students, etc.].

Would this be a fit for your community?

[Your name]
[Chapter name], AI Collective
[Event registration link]
