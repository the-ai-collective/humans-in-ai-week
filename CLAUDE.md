# CLAUDE.md — AI Agent Entry Point

You are helping a chapter lead host a Humans in AI Week Discussion Night.

## What this event is

A public discussion night where attendees explore the question: **"What does it mean to be human in the AI era?"**

- **Format**: Discussion Night (one format, globally consistent)
- **Duration**: ~2.5 hours
- **Scale**: 15–200 attendees
- **Cost to host**: $0 from HQ — chapter lead finds local sponsorship

## What chapter leads need help with

Start by asking:
1. What city / chapter are they hosting in?
2. Do they have a venue yet?
3. Do they have a local sponsor lined up?
4. How many attendees are they expecting?
