# Pre-Event Timeline

A 6-week countdown for chapter leads. Adjust based on when you start.

---

## 6 weeks out

- [ ] Confirm you're hosting — register your event on the global Luma calendar (link TBD)
- [ ] Choose your date within June 1–7
- [ ] Start venue search — prioritize a free host space before looking at paid venues
- [ ] Identify 3–5 sponsor targets (see [`sponsorship/pitch.md`](../sponsorship/pitch.md))

---

## 4–5 weeks out

- [ ] Lock the venue
- [ ] Send sponsor outreach (see [`sponsorship/outreach.md`](../sponsorship/outreach.md))
- [ ] Open RSVPs — use Luma, Eventbrite, or a simple form
- [ ] Post your first social announcement (see [`marketing/social-copy.md`](../marketing/social-copy.md))
- [ ] Make a copy of the presentation and customize your 2 slides (see [`presentation/slides.md`](../presentation/slides.md))

---

## 2–3 weeks out

- [ ] Confirm sponsor (follow up if no response — see [`sponsorship/outreach.md`](../sponsorship/outreach.md))
- [ ] Send invites to your core community (see [`marketing/attendee-invite.md`](../marketing/attendee-invite.md))
- [ ] Post second social reminder
- [ ] Decide: app or printed question cards? (see [`app/README.md`](../app/README.md))
- [ ] Order or prep name tag supplies (colored labels or dot stickers)

---

## 1 week out

- [ ] Send event reminder to your RSVP list
- [ ] Confirm venue logistics — access time, AV, layout
- [ ] Confirm sponsor logistics — food/drink delivery, setup window
- [ ] Post final social push
- [ ] Do a run-through of the presentation

---

## Day before

- [ ] Print question cards (if not using the app)
- [ ] Prep name tags by color (see [`event/format.md`](./format.md) for the color system)
- [ ] Send final reminder to attendees
- [ ] Test the presentation on your laptop

---

## Day of

See [`event/run-of-show.md`](./run-of-show.md).

---

## After the event

- [ ] Send follow-up to attendees with [hiai.com](https://hiai.com) pledge link
- [ ] Post a recap photo or reflection on social
- [ ] Submit post-event report to HQ (link TBD)
